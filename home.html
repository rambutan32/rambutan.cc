rambutan

home | documentation | forums | repositories

Rambutan is a volunteer-run open embedded engineering project to make development with the STM32 family of ARM Cortex-M3/M4 microcontrollers more enjoyable. It started[0] as a continuation of Leaflab's "Maple" open hardware development board product line, and in particular the libmaple C library written for that hardware.

Development and planning are still in early stages. Jump in the forums to take part, or watch this space for future updates!

[0]: well, more like "is starting", as of September 2014
